Our Company.

PelEng Consulting Engineers is a professional engineering consultancy offering Architects, Developers, Builders and Home Owners over 20 years practical engineering expertise in structural and civil engineering design.

PelEng Consulting Engineers was established in 1994 and has expanded since to provide structural and civil consulting services throughout N.S.W. The company has the technical and practical ability to handle projects ranging from small alterations & additions to high-density multi storey unit developments. We specialise in low to medium density residential developments, including prestige houses and multi storey unit developments.

Our Services.

Structural Engineering

We combine innovative design along with a holistic approach to structural design. An iterative method is employed in delivering functional, easy-to-build and feasible designs, catering to a wide array of project specifications and client needs. We utilise the latest in design methods and modelling techniques in ensuring our designs remain optimised and compliant with all Australian building codes and standards.

Extensive Expertise

Our team of registered engineers and drafters are highly skilled in designing and drafting all types of structural projects, including Architecturally Designed Projects, Luxury Dwellings, New Residential Dwellings, Townhouses, First Floor Additions, Alterations & Additions, Extensions and Domestic Swimming Pools.

Innovative Solutions

PelEng Consulting Engineers is committed to provide its clients with innovative, reliable, and affordable engineering designs to achieve client's utmost satisfaction. We have extensive expertise in concrete, structural steel, timber and masonry structures. Our detailed documentation provides ease of construction that meet our clients' needs, Australian Standards concordance, regulatory requirements and safety requirements.

Construction Phase

Qualified engineers carry out the inspections during the construction phase of projects to ensure the construction complies with the specifications detailed at the design stage.

Design Phase

PelEng Consulting Engineers incorporates in the design phase the latest building material technology in the market to produce major cost savings in construction. Simplicity in design is the key for economical buildings. Simple designs are produced by PelEng that intuitively understand their structures and respect the role of specialists and teamwork.

The expertise of the practice is enhanced by utilising the latest in computer technology that covers different aspects of engineering design, as well as the latest in engineering techniques.

Foundation Design

Design of all foundation structures and detailing of all associated works to Australian Standards including Slab on-grade, Waffle-pod slabs, Strip and pad footings, Cast in-situ (augured) piles, Driven Piles and Screw piles.


Reinforced Concrete Design

Structural design, reinforcement layouts and drafting services for all concrete members to Australian Standards including Beams, Slabs, Columns and Walls (sheer, core and lift-shafts.

Structural Steel Design

Steel member design for all structural steel beams, columns, trusses, as well as connection design and all associated drafting and detailing.

Timber Design

Design of timber structural members and relevant connection detailing to Australian Standards for all Roof, Floor and Wall members including all Bearers, Joists, Rafters, Posts, Stud Walls, Hanging Beams, Strutting Beams and Purlins

Certifications and Approvals Services

We offer full engineering certification of all proposed and existing structural works including staged construction, alterations and additions, extensions and fit-outs on existing structures.

Dilapidation Reports

Dilapidation Reports are carried out to record the condition of a property or asset at a particular point in time. The purpose of a Dilapidation Report is to avoid a dispute or costly litigation at the completion of construction works by establishing a record of the condition of a building and/or infrastructure prior to construction.

Our experience and extensive knowledge of structures and construction types enable us to pinpoint the parts of buildings most vulnerable to likely movement or other damage.

PelEng Consulting Engineers can provide an independent detailed photographic record with commentary for each item in our Reports. The dilapidation report ensures our detailed walk through of the property is recorded and compiled with extreme thoroughness to make sure all defects are recorded and photographed.
