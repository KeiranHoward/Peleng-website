About Us

Our Company.

PelEng Consulting Engineers is a professional engineering consultancy offering Architects, Developers, Builders and Home Owners over 20 years practical engineering expertise in structural and civil engineering design.

PelEng Consulting Engineers was established in 1994 and has expanded since to provide structural and civil consulting services throughout N.S.W. The company has the technical and practical ability to handle projects ranging from small alterations & additions to high-density multi storey unit developments. We specialise in low to medium density residential developments, including prestige houses and multi storey unit developments.

Years Experience

Design Philosophy.

Our design emphasis is to provide efficient, cost effective engineering solutions for our clients, giving consideration to the needs of individuals and tailoring our services accordingly

Company Goals.

PelEng strives to provide innovative, simple, economical and elegant engineering solutions to a wide range of projects to the satisfaction of the client, designer and builder. This generally leads to superior structural performance with speed and cost saving in construction.

We aim to incorporate in the design phase the latest building material technology in the market to produce major cost savings in construction.

PelEng endeavours to take great care to understand the requirements of our clients in an effort to provide the most practical and cost-efficient solutions.

The expertise of the practice is enhanced by utilising the latest in computer technology that cover different aspects of engineering design, as well as the latest in engineering techniques to achieve the highest engineering standards on every project, regardless of size.
